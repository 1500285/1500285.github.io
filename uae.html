The United Arab Emirates(UAE) is a Middle eastern country located in Western Asia bordering Oman and Saudi Arabia. UAE’s largest export is natural gas, they are the seventh largest oil reserve and their economy is heavily dependant on it. The UAE also gets a lot of revenue from tourism, as they are home to many great attractions we will discuss later. The country is slated to host the World’s Fair in 2020. Since the country is about as far south as Texas, temperatures tend to be very high, climbing to about 113℉ in August.


The UAE is home to two major cities: Abu Dhabi and Dubai. Dubai is the larger and more well-known of the two. Dubai is recovering from an economic crisis currently, and their prosperity is evident through beautiful architecture and towering skyscrapers. Dubai houses the Burj Khalifa, the tallest man-made structure in the entire world, at over 160 stories tall. Additionally, the city is home to a luxury hotel called the Burj al Arab. Abu Dhabi, on the other hand, is the nation's capital. The city is mostly known for Ferrari World, and indoor theme park home to the world's fastest roller coaster, Formula Rossa.
