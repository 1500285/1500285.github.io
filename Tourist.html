Dubai is also home to the world's tallest building: the Burj Khalifa.


The Burj Khalifa is, technically speaking, the world's tallest man-made structure. It stands over 160 stories tall in the Dubai Skyline. The building has observation decks on floors 148, 125, and 124. On the very top of the building is a transmissions spire.
